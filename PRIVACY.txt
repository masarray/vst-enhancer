ARSONKUPIK PRIVACY NOTICE

Effective date: 17 July 2026

Tutorial Mas Ari / MasArray is responsible for personal information that it
receives directly for ArSonKuPik activation, fulfilment, or support. GitHub and
any future authorised Merchant of Record process information under their own
privacy terms for the services they provide.

1. LOCAL AUDIO PROCESSING

ArSonKuPik is designed to process audio locally on the user's computer. It does
not intentionally collect or transmit audio, DAW projects, parameter values,
preset selections, licence codes, crash analytics, advertising identifiers, or
usage analytics during normal operation.

2. OFFLINE ACTIVATION

The Computer Request ID is generated locally. It is not transmitted
automatically. It is shared with Tutorial Mas Ari / MasArray or an authorised
activation service only when the user chooses to copy and send it to request an
activation code. Activation codes are validated locally and no permanent
background licence service is required.

3. MANUAL UPDATE CHECK

ArSonKuPik makes a network request only when the user manually presses the update
check control. The request reads a small public release manifest hosted through
the project's public website or GitHub infrastructure. The hosting provider may
receive ordinary connection information such as an IP address, timestamp, user
agent, and request path under its own privacy terms. ArSonKuPik does not append
audio, project, licence, preset, or analytics data to that request.

4. WEBSITE LANGUAGE PREFERENCE

The public website can store one item in the user's browser local storage:

  askp-language = en or id

This value remembers the selected English or Bahasa Indonesia interface. It is
not an advertising identifier, is not sent by the website to Tutorial Mas Ari /
MasArray, and can be removed by clearing site data in the browser. The website
does not intentionally install advertising or analytics cookies.

5. WEBSITE DOWNLOADS AND PUBLIC FEEDBACK

Downloads, GitHub Pages visits, GitHub Releases, and GitHub Issues are provided
through GitHub. GitHub may process connection, account, cookie, and activity data
under its own privacy policy.

Public issue reports are visible to other users. Do not post activation codes,
Computer Request IDs, order records, proof-of-purchase documents, private project
files, customer audio, personal information, or confidential material in a
public issue. Security vulnerabilities must be reported privately according to
SECURITY.md.

6. EVALUATION DOWNLOAD AND PAYMENT SEPARATION

No payment card is required for the 365-day evaluation, and ArSonKuPik cannot
charge a user automatically when the evaluation ends. Availability of the public
evaluation download is separate from availability of paid checkout.

Paid checkout is not enabled until an authorised provider can display the seller
identity, final amount and currency, tax, payment methods, refund information,
privacy notice, and provider terms before payment.

When a user voluntarily completes an authorised purchase, the Merchant of Record
will process payment details, tax, fraud checks, refunds, receipts, and related
data under its own terms and privacy notice. Tutorial Mas Ari / MasArray should
receive only information reasonably needed for fulfilment and support, such as
order ID, customer contact information, product, payment status, refund status,
and licence entitlement. ArSonKuPik and its public repository do not store
payment-card data.

7. SUPPORT AND ACTIVATION RECORDS

When a user voluntarily contacts the Licensor or requests activation, the
supplied message, contact details, proof of purchase, Computer Request ID,
activation history, and troubleshooting information may be retained as
reasonably necessary to:

- fulfil the purchase and administer the licence;
- provide support and investigate reproducible defects;
- prevent fraud, duplicated entitlement, and licence abuse;
- maintain accounting and transaction records; and
- comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

Retention depends on the purpose, licence lifetime, support history, transaction
status, and applicable legal or accounting requirements. Information that is no
longer reasonably required should be deleted, anonymised, or securely archived
as appropriate. Do not send audio, projects, identity documents, or other
sensitive data unless specifically requested and a suitable private transfer
method has been agreed.

8. INTERNATIONAL SERVICE PROVIDERS

GitHub and any future checkout, email, storage, or support provider may process
connection or transaction information in countries other than the user's own,
according to their infrastructure and privacy terms. Any future checkout must
identify the provider and applicable privacy information before payment.

9. NO AUTOMATED AUDIO PROFILING

ArSonKuPik does not use uploaded audio, project content, or usage analytics to
build advertising profiles or make automated decisions about users. A future
Merchant of Record may perform payment-fraud checks under its own disclosed
terms.

This notice may be updated when the product, hosting, sales, activation, or
support process changes. Material changes will be published with a new effective
