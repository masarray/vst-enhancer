Project-safe licensing

Full editing for a year. Playback stays safe.

Days 1–335: all presets, knobs, A/B controls, bypass, and utility trims remain fully editable.

Final 30 days: a small warning badge shows the remaining editable-beta time.

After day 365: the current sound, meters, saved settings, and project playback continue, while a dark activation panel blocks editing.

Offline activation: copy the Computer Request ID, send it to Mas Ari after purchase, then paste the returned hardware-bound activation code.

No constant internet connection, licence server, or account login is required.
