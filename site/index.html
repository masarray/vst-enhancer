Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you download.

These answers are a plain-language overview. The EULA and privacy notice remain the controlling public documents for the evaluation.

What is ArSonKuPik?

ArSonKuPik is a Windows VST3 plug-in and standalone audio enhancer for mastering, mix bus, tracks, vocals, podcasts and creative processing.

Is every preset and control really free for 365 days?

Yes. Full editing is available for 365 days from first launch on each computer, subject to the EULA.

Do I need an account or payment card?

No. The evaluation starts without registration, a payment card or a checkout process.

Will I be charged automatically?

No. The evaluation does not collect a card, create a subscription, renew or charge automatically.

Am I required to buy after the free year?

No. There is no purchase obligation. Optional activation is relevant only if you want to keep changing presets and controls after the evaluation.

What happens after 365 days?

Existing projects are designed to continue processing in project-safe read-only mode. Preset selection and editing controls require optional activation.

Can I use it for paid work during evaluation?

Yes, for personal and commercial audio-production work during evaluation, subject to the EULA. You retain rights in the audio and projects you create.

Does it upload my audio or projects?

No intentional upload of audio, DAW projects, parameter values, presets, licence codes, crash analytics or usage analytics is built into normal operation.

Which systems and formats are supported?

The current public target is Windows 10/11 64-bit, VST3 and Standalone. macOS, Linux, VST2, AAX and Audio Unit are not currently distributed.

Why does SmartScreen show a warning?

The current package is unsigned, so Windows may show an unknown-publisher or reputation warning. Download only from the official release, verify SHA-256 and keep normal antivirus protection enabled.

Is every DAW guaranteed to work?

No universal compatibility guarantee is made. Evaluate ArSonKuPik in your own DAW, driver, sample rate and buffer settings before critical use.

What does optional activation support?

It keeps editing available for the purchased product generation and helps fund independent development, JUCE licensing as applicable, testing, documentation, user support and trusted Windows distribution. It is a purchase for activation rights, not a donation.

Where should I report a bug?

Use the public GitHub issue form for non-confidential bugs. Never post activation codes, Computer Request IDs, private projects, customer audio or personal data.
