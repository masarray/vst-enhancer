Installation and verification — four steps
  1. Download only from the official release.
  2. Compare the installer SHA-256 with SHA256SUMS.txt.
  3. Run the installer and keep the default VST3 location.
  4. Rescan VST3 in your DAW when needed.
Get-FileHash .\ArSonKuPik-v0.5.12-Windows-x64-Setup.exe -Algorithm SHA256 Open SHA256SUMS.txt

The current package is not commercially code-signed yet, so SmartScreen may show an unknown-publisher warning. Keep antivirus enabled and stop if the checksum differs.

Frequently asked questions

The essentials before you download.

What is ArSonKuPik?

A Windows VST3 plug-in and Standalone audio enhancer for master, mix bus, tracks, vocals, podcasts and creative processing.

VST3 or Standalone?

VST3 runs inside a compatible DAW. Standalone runs as a separate Windows application.

Is everything really free for 365 days?

Yes. Every preset and editing control is available without an account, card, subscription, automatic charge or purchase obligation, subject to the EULA.

What happens after 365 days?

Existing projects are designed to keep processing in project-safe read-only mode. Optional activation is relevant only for continued editing.

Can I use it for paid work?

Yes, during evaluation for personal and commercial audio-production work, subject to the EULA. Your audio and projects remain yours.

Does it upload audio or track usage?

Normal operation has no intentional audio upload, crash analytics or usage analytics. Update checking happens only when you request it.

Which systems are supported?

Windows 10/11 64-bit, VST3 and Standalone. Test your own DAW, driver, sample rate and buffer before critical work.

Why can SmartScreen show a warning?

The current package is unsigned. Download only from the official release, verify SHA-256 and keep normal antivirus protection enabled.
