Beginner-friendly setup

Download, verify, install, and start safely.

Use only the official release page. The current build is unsigned, so Windows SmartScreen may show a reputation warning even when the file is unchanged.

1

Open the official release

Download only when this page reports that distribution is enabled. Avoid mirrors and re-uploaded packages.

2

Verify SHA-256

Compare the downloaded file with SHA256SUMS.txt from the same GitHub Release before installing.

3

Install VST3 or Standalone

The installer places the VST3 in the standard Windows plug-in location. Standalone can run without a DAW.

4

Scan in your DAW

Open your DAW plug-in manager, rescan VST3 plug-ins, then load ArSonKuPik on a track, bus, or master as appropriate.

Windows SmartScreen

A warning does not replace file verification.

Because the installer is not code-signed yet, Windows may display “Windows protected your PC.” Confirm that the file came from the official release, verify its SHA-256 checksum, and do not continue when the checksum differs.

Get-FileHash .\ArSonKuPik-Setup.exe -Algorithm SHA256

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you install.

These answers explain the product in plain language. The EULA, purchase terms, privacy notice, and checkout terms remain the controlling legal documents.

What is ArSonKuPik?

ArSonKuPik is a Windows VST3 plug-in and standalone audio enhancer designed for mastering, mix bus, individual tracks, vocals, podcasts, and creative processing.

Is the one-year evaluation really free?

Yes. Full editing is available for 365 days from first launch on each computer. No card, account, subscription, or automatic payment is required to start.

Will I be charged automatically after 365 days?

No. ArSonKuPik has no payment card to charge and does not create a subscription. You pay only when you voluntarily complete an official checkout.

What happens when the evaluation ends?

The software is designed to enter project-safe read-only mode. Existing projects, saved settings, meters, automation playback, and audio processing may continue, while preset selection and editing controls require activation.

Will old projects be muted or filled with noise?

The software does not intentionally mute audio, inject noise, or force true bypass solely because the evaluation ended. Always keep project backups because beta software can still have defects or compatibility issues.

Can I use it for paid or commercial work during evaluation?

Yes, for personal and commercial audio-production work during the evaluation, subject to the EULA. You retain rights in the audio and projects you create.

Does it require an account or permanent internet connection?

No account or permanent connection is required for normal audio processing or offline activation. A network request is made only when you manually request an update check.

Does ArSonKuPik upload my audio or projects?

No intentional upload of audio, DAW projects, parameter values, presets, licence codes, crash analytics, or usage analytics is built into normal operation.

Which systems and plug-in formats are supported?

The current public target is Windows 10/11 64-bit, VST3, and Standalone. macOS, Linux, VST2, AAX, and Audio Unit are not currently distributed.

Why does Windows SmartScreen show a warning?

The current installer is unsigned, so Windows may not yet recognise its publisher reputation. Download only from the official release and verify the SHA-256 checksum before running it.

How do I verify the download?

Open PowerShell and run Get-FileHash with SHA256, then compare the result with SHA256SUMS.txt from the same official GitHub Release. Do not install when the values differ.

How does paid activation work?

After an official purchase, you provide the locally generated Computer Request ID through the authorised fulfilment process. You receive a hardware-bound activation code that is validated locally.

How many computers can I activate?

One purchase may be used on up to two active computers owned or controlled by the customer. Codes may not be shared, resold, or published.

What if I replace or repair my computer?

A reasonable reissue may be provided after proof of purchase and an abuse review. Reissue can require deactivation of the previous computer.

Does USD 25 include every future version?

No. The published offer covers perpetual editing for the purchased v0.5 product generation. It does not guarantee every future major version, operating system, DAW, feature, or compatibility update.

What is the refund policy?

Refund eligibility and procedure will be presented by the Merchant of Record at checkout. A refund or chargeback may end the related activation entitlement and block new activations or reissues, subject to applicable law.

Is every DAW guaranteed to work?

No universal compatibility guarantee is made. Hosts, drivers, devices, and configurations vary. Evaluate ArSonKuPik in your own DAW, sample rate, and buffer settings before relying on it for critical work.

Where should I report a problem?

Use the public GitHub issue form for non-confidential bugs and feedback. Never post activation codes, Computer Request IDs, private projects, customer audio, or personal data in a public issue.
