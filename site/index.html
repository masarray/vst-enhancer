Installation and verification — four steps
  1. Download only from the official release.
  2. Compare the installer SHA-256 with SHA256SUMS.txt.
  3. Run the installer and keep the standard VST3 location.
  4. Rescan VST3 plug-ins in your DAW when needed.
Get-FileHash .\ArSonKuPik-v0.5.13-Windows-x64-Setup.exe -Algorithm SHA256Open SHA256SUMS.txt

The current package is not commercially code-signed yet, so SmartScreen may show an unknown-publisher warning. Keep antivirus enabled and stop if the checksum differs.

Frequently asked questions

The essentials before you download.

What is ArSonKuPik?

A focused, musical Windows VST3 and Standalone audio enhancer for master, mix bus, tracks, vocals, podcasts, creative processing and everyday listening.

What makes Mas Ari Signature special?

It is the flagship balance created to make familiar music feel more alive, reveal fine detail and produce a convincing live-like dimension without requiring a complex chain.

VST3 or Standalone?

VST3 runs inside a compatible DAW. Standalone runs as a separate Windows application for supported audio-device workflows.

How long is full editing available?

Every preset and editing control is available for 365 days from first launch on each computer, without an account, card, subscription, automatic charge or purchase obligation.

What happens after full editing ends?

Existing projects, saved values, meters, automation playback and audio processing are designed to continue in project-safe read-only mode. Optional activation is relevant only for continued editing.

Can I use it for paid work?

Yes, during the evaluation for personal and commercial audio-production work, subject to the EULA. Your audio and projects remain yours.

Does it upload audio or track usage?

Normal operation has no intentional audio upload, crash analytics or usage analytics. Update checking happens only when you request it.

Why can SmartScreen show a warning?

The current package is unsigned. Download only from the official release, verify SHA-256 and keep normal antivirus protection enabled.
