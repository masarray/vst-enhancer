Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you install.

These answers are a plain-language overview. The EULA, purchase terms, privacy notice and any future checkout terms control the licence and transaction.

What is ArSonKuPik?

ArSonKuPik is a Windows VST3 plug-in and standalone audio enhancer designed for mastering, mix bus, individual tracks, vocals, podcasts and creative processing.

Is the 365-day evaluation really free?

Yes. Full editing is available for 365 days from first launch on each computer. No card, account, subscription or automatic payment is required to start.

Will I be charged automatically?

No. The evaluation does not collect a payment card and does not create a subscription. Payment can occur only if you voluntarily complete an authorised checkout when one is available.

What happens after 365 days?

The software is designed to enter project-safe read-only mode. Existing projects, saved settings, meters, automation playback and audio processing may continue, while preset selection and editing controls require activation.

Can I use it for paid work during evaluation?

Yes, for personal and commercial audio-production work during the evaluation, subject to the EULA. You retain rights in the audio and projects you create.

Does it upload my audio or projects?

No intentional upload of audio, DAW projects, parameter values, presets, licence codes, crash analytics or usage analytics is built into normal operation.

Which systems and formats are supported?

The current public target is Windows 10/11 64-bit, VST3 and Standalone. macOS, Linux, VST2, AAX and Audio Unit are not currently distributed.

Why does SmartScreen show a warning?

The current package is unsigned, so Windows may show an unknown-publisher or reputation warning. Download only from the official release, verify SHA-256 and keep normal antivirus protection enabled.

Is paid activation available now?

The public evaluation download is available. Paid checkout is a separate process and is not enabled until an authorised provider can show seller identity, payment, tax, privacy and refund terms before purchase.

How many computers can a purchase activate?

A completed purchase may be used on up to two active computers owned or controlled by the customer. Codes may not be shared, resold or published.

Is every DAW guaranteed to work?

No universal compatibility guarantee is made. Hosts, drivers, devices and configurations vary. Evaluate ArSonKuPik in your own DAW, sample rate and buffer settings before critical use.

Where should I report a bug?

Use the public GitHub issue form for non-confidential bugs. Never post activation codes, Computer Request IDs, private projects, customer audio or personal data in a public issue.

How should I report a security problem?

Use the repository security policy and private vulnerability-reporting channel. Do not disclose exploit details, licence-system weaknesses, customer data or suspected key exposure in a public issue.
